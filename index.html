About Me

I'm Gidi Rabi. A second year Computer Science student in Ariel University, and I'm thrilled to be diving into the professional world. For the past 2 years, I've been studying Computer Science, and I've been absolutely loving it. From coding to algorithms, I've been fascinated by the endless possibilities also getting some unique characteristics like problem-solving and thinking out of the box.

As I step into this new chapter, I'll admit, I don't have all the answers about where I'll be in five years. Whether it's coding, problem-solving, or just embracing the unexpected twists and turns along the way, count me in!

More than anything, I'm excited to be part of a team, to collaborate, to share ideas, and to create something truly special together. So, here's to new beginnings, endless possibilities, and the exciting journey ahead!
